About Me

I am currently in a first-ever rotational program at W. W. Grainger. I opted for a rotational experience over selecting and staying with one team because I believe that by regularly experiencing new teams and management styles, that I would foster new collaboration networks between teams allowing for Grainger to move faster and be more productive. I am also working with the necessary teams and Human Resources at Grainger to ensure the program will become an option for future new-hires to partake in should they feel inclined.

In my rotational experience at Grainger, I opted for a variety of different roles in different teams, in order to prove I can quickly create and drive value for Grainger through many roles (Administrator, Developer, Engineer, etc). Through rapidly experience several different roles, I learned how to leverage existing knowledge and resources in order to quickly drive value (as each rotation is only 6 months long).

Prior to joining Grainger, I studied Information Systems/Information Technology and Business Process Management at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. In addition to my studies, I was also the lead Network Technician for University Housing, where I managed the endpoints and campus owned network for thousands of students. Finally, I developed the software and hardware needed for a robotic distance camera that allowed studies who have vision impairment to see the board in lecture halls.

Outside of Grainger, I enjoy studying Mandarin Chinese and am looking to get involved with 3D printing so I can complete more personal projects that involve hardware in addition to software.
